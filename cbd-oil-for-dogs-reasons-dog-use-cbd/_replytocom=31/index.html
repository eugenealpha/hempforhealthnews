Is CBD Oil For Dogs? 3 Reasons your dog might use CBD

Posted on November 13, 2017Author Catherine Gable Johanson 2 Comments on Is CBD Oil For Dogs? 3 Reasons your dog might use CBD

Is CBD Oil For Dogs with ANXIETY?

National Public Radio did an article about CBD and dogs with anxiety.  They found while some vets have found CBD to be a great treatment, many vets acknowledge that we just don’t have long-term studies on how CBD might affect canines.

Since CBD is non-toxic and has been tested in dogs in short-term studies with no negative effects owners that feel a more “natural” approach than giving your nervous family member a medication, CBD oil for dogs is a great place to start. As long as you get your dog’s CBD from an outstanding company it’s a pretty safe way to try and ease some anxiety that dogs can get from fireworks, strange surroundings, thunderstorms and even unknown household guests.

CBD OIL FOR DOGS WITH PAIN

Here’s the great news; for owners that feel a more “natural” approach than giving your older family member pain relievers, CBD oil for dogs is a great place to start. As long as you get your dog’s CBD from an outstanding company it’s a pretty safe way to try and ease some suffering we all get as we get older.

It’s also a great addition to those pills the vet gives you. Some CBD oil for dogs users have found they can cut back or even stop other pills after starting their dog on CBD.

Of course consulting with your vet before starting any change with your dog is a great idea. While the news is spreading fast don’t be surprised if your vet hasn’t heard or thinks CBD is untested.

CBD Oil For Dogs with Seizures

This is perhaps the best-known benefit of CBD. We’ve heard stories of it stopping seizures when nothing else would in both people and humans. If you have a dog with seizures get your vet to approve some CBD as soon as possible.
